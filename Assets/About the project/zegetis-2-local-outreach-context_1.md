# Zeget'is 2.0 Local Outreach - Project Context

**Last Updated:** 2025-01-29  
**Status:** Design complete, ready for prospect testing phase

---

## Project Overview

Launching local video/photo production outreach for Zeget'is targeting companies in Flanders with postal codes starting with **28XX**. Moving from working through production houses and big brands to direct relationships with local businesses.

**Core positioning:** "High-end production quality (normally reserved for companies like Telenet, Primus, Stad Mechelen) now accessible to local businesses in 28XX."

---

## Key Decisions Made

### Target Audience
- **Geographic:** All Flanders postal codes starting with 28
- **Size:** 5-50 employees
- **Budget indicators:**
  - Existing marketing/video content
  - Marketing team or job postings
  - Recent growth/awards/news
  - Quality website with professional domain
- **Exclusions:**
  - Video/photo production competitors
  - Zero online presence (no website)

### Outreach Strategy

**Email structure (finalized):**
1. Rotating hook (6 variations - see design doc)
2. Clear offer: "Ik maak video en foto content voor bedrijven—van productvideo's tot bedrijfscontent en testimonials."
3. Why local story: "10 jaar heb ik productiehuizen en grote merken gediend. Nu wil ik werken met bedrijven hier in de buurt—direct, zonder tussenpersonen, met volledige creatieve controle."
4. Personalization line (their growth/news/website quality)
5. 28% Q1 2026 offer
6. CTA with calendar link to /28xx landing page

**6 Email Hooks (rotate for A/B testing):**
- A: "Video zoals Deloitte het krijgt, maar dan voor bedrijven in 2830."
- B: "De kwaliteit die normaal enkel grote merken kunnen betalen, nu beschikbaar voor lokale bedrijven."
- C: "Tien jaar heb ik voor grote namen gewerkt. Nu breng ik diezelfde kwaliteit naar bedrijven hier in de buurt."
- D: "Waarom zouden enkel multinationals recht hebben op goede beelden?"
- E: "Zit je te kijken naar wat je concurrent op Instagram post en denk je: waarom doen wij dat niet?"
- F: "Ooit jaloers geweest op de video's van een concurrent? Niet meer nodig."

**Volume:**
- Week 1: 50 emails (launch burst)
- Week 2+: 25 emails/week

**Tone requirements:**
- Down-to-earth Flemish (not Dutch/Netherlands)
- Informal, authentic
- Subtle humor (goal: make them smile at least once)
- NEVER salesy

### Website Strategy

**Main site:**
- Sabato.be aesthetic (refined, editorial, high-level Belgian design)
- Value/offer-first (NOT portfolio-first)
- Clear CTAs throughout
- Future: Blog for local SEO

**Landing page /28xx:**
- A/B test two versions:
  - Version A: Hook → Offer → Story → Portfolio → Calendar
  - Version B: Hook → Portfolio → Story → Offer → Calendar
- 30-second popup for calendar booking
- Google Calendar integration (two options: 30-min intake OR direct shoot booking)

**Build tool:** Antigravity

### Automation Tools

**Prospect finding:**
- Claude Cowork (manual, conversational, testing phase)
- OR Antigravity (scheduled automation once workflow validated)

**Email sending:**
- Claude Cowork drafts → Daan approves → Cowork sends via Gmail

**Workflow:**
1. Find prospects (25-50/week)
2. Draft personalized emails with rotating hooks
3. Get batch approval
4. Send via Gmail
5. Track opens/clicks/bookings

## Key Principles & Constraints

### From Daan's Brand Voice
- Short, direct communication (dyslexia/ADD considerations)
- Authentic over polished
- "Hire me for ME" philosophy
- 10 minutes prep saves major revisions later
- Iterate fast, adjust as you go

### From This Project
- Speed over perfection (1-2 week launch)
- Test everything (hooks, landing pages, criteria)
- Local focus (this is about 28XX presence, not national scaling)
- Direct relationships (no intermediaries)
- Q1 2026 urgency (28% discount deadline)
